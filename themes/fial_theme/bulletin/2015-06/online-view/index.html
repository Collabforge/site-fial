Welcome to the FIAL Bulletin - June 2015
Message from Chairman

I am delighted to inform you that we have submitted the Food and Agribusiness Growth Centre Application to Minister Ian Macfarlane. These past few months have been busy collecting your feedback and using this to inform the Application. We believe the industry is well placed to take advantage of the many identified opportunities locally and overseas.

Continue reading the full Chairmans message here.
Message from Managing Director

The consultation process and the meetings with the 300 plus industry stakeholders have provided strong insights into the challenges facing the industry and also refined our thinking into how we overcome them. The overwhelming support and responses from the industry have been tremendous and we are very excited at the next phase of our journey. On behalf of the team and I, we would like to thank you for your continuing encouragement and contribution in helping our industry transform.
