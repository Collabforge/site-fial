During 2014 FIAL developed numerous programs on information, tools and connections of relevance to the industry. We are pleased that these programs have assisted both individuals and companies in delivering value. Sharing outcomes and learnings from FIAL's initiatives is important to us because we want to keep you informed.

We are delighted to share FIAL's Annual Report 2013-14 which we hope provides new insights about market and other opportunities. Some of the key highlights in this report include establishing a database of ~1500 stakeholder organisations, of which 80% are SMEs; developing an interactive website with over 5000 visitors where 42% are returning visitors; instigating over 40 workshops on various topics such as channel readiness and food futures; and starting 30+ collaborative projects with a majority of these on innovation. We hope you enjoy reading our annual report.

2015 brings a new phase for FIAL. The Department has agreed to provide FIAL with bridging funding arrangements so they can be involved in the facilitation process to develop an Industry Growth Centre proposal for consideration by Government. This means over the next three months, we will continue to provide information to support your strategic growth opportunities and also invite you to participate in our innovation and market access initiatives.
 
Peter Schutz - Chairman

In November we hosted the Group Procurement Manager for T Choithram & Sons, a leading supermarket retailer and distributor across four key Middle East markets. In total 60 buyer/supplier meetings were held and over 100 Export Managers attended Middle East market presentations in Perth, Adelaide, Melbourne, Sydney and Brisbane. The buyer indicated that as a result of the targeted company selection approach, Choithrams is likely to trial products from 60% of those he met; indeed, initial trial orders for many have already been placed. In addition, 15 brands were identified by the buyer from the supermarket visits in each city and negotiations have already commenced with many of these.

The next installment of the Inbound Buyer Mission is planned for March 2015. To be considered for buyer/supplier meetings and market presentations, please register your supplier profile on the FIAL eCatalogue.
