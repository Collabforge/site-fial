FIAL believes that the success of the agribusiness, food and beverage industry will be largely determined by how well we collaborate across the whole food value chain and connect effectively to export markets. There is tremendous power in collaboration to accelerate solutions and growth. Collaboration underpins everything we do in FIAL.
 
Thanks to the collaboration with our many partnerships with science centres, government, learning institutions, universities, associations and specialists across the industry, we have developed several programs, events, tools and information focused on the specific needs of the industry.
 
Collaboration also brings businesses together to share skills, resources and knowledge. It fosters connections, relationships, identifies opportunities and drives innovation.
 
I urge you to consider investing some time in your busy schedules to participate in our new programs available now, and over the next months.
 
Gain immediate benefits and experience a new way of working and ideas sharing, and see for yourself the power of collaboration across diverse networks.
 
By working together, we have the opportunity to improve productivity, profitability for your businesses and resilience for the whole food and beverage industry.
 
Peter Schutz
Chairman
Time away from your business is time not spent on your business, which is why I would like to thank those who have taken the time to meet representatives from FIAL over the past months. We have met over 400 people across the country in 22 cities and regional centres, and had great conversations listening to your business challenges and needs. Thanks to your insights, we have developed a range of programs with our partners to build capabilities and connect you to opportunities.
 
Many of you asked for a copy of the presentation at the Meet FIAL events and we would be happy to email you a copy. Please send us an email at info@fial.com.au.
 
The industry has the resources, talent and drive to be global leaders in the supply of food and beverage, but we need to work together to overcome the barriers and knowledge gaps that exist. Industry collaboration can accelerate the creation of tangible solutions - finding the right contact, information, skill or support, which can lead to a path that seemed impossible at the onset.
 
The team at FIAL appreciates the support we receive from across the country and looks forward to working with you in the future as we roll out even more initiatives to benefit Australia's food and beverage industry.
 
Mirjana Prica
Managing Director
